Services

Our main activity is transport, but we also offer services like customs mediating, warehousing the goods, delivery to customer ware-house with high respect of the customer needs. Transport relations that we usually do are: Macedonia, Albania, Serbia, Kosovo to Italy, Switzerland, Germany. The most to Italy and from Italy to Switzerland, Germany and conversely.

We do full, collective and special transport of goods which we execute with most sophisticated trucks and we constantly increase our vehicle fleet.
Through the years we proudly walk forward and we notice permanent increase in supplying new and modern vehicles, and also with employees who has knowledge and capacity to answer to every new challenge. Quality and professionalism were our main aims from the beginning of company establishing, and still are.

Today, our company became one of the most professional and successful companies for international transport in our country and abroad. With pleasure we can offer you our services, ready to accomplish all future challenges.
